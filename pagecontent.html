Welcome to KamiKita Community

KamiKita is a community center in Banda Aceh. We would love to
provide a safe space for everyone to come, share, network or just
for coffee. we belive in empowering people to work together and
be a part of a community that builds one another.
We want tointentionally be a bridge promoting literacy (health/spot, farming,
social, financial, literary, emotional and more) in the spirit of
Bhinneka Tunggal Ika or unity in diversity.
